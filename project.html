Footbal Transfer Market Analysis Using Power BI

This Power BI project aimed to strengthen a data analyst portfolio and enhance Power BI proficiency by analyzing football transfer market data across the top 5 European leagues from 2005 to 2022 and display it in dashbourd. The project involved data cleaning, transformation, modeling, and visualization to derive actionable insights. I took the dataset from Kaggle , This platform is known as sharing datasets by others.
