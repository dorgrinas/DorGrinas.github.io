Power BI project

This Power BI project aimed to strengthen a data analyst portfolio and enhance Power BI proficiency by analyzing soccer transfer market data across the top 5 European leagues from 2005 to 2022. The project involved data cleaning, transformation, modeling, and visualization to derive actionable insights. Through this project, I sought to not only gain valuable insights into the soccer transfer market but also to practice and refine my Power BI skills.
